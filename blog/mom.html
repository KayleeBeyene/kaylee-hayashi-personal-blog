Amazing article

Kaylee Beyene
18 March, 2022

My mom is a great lady. She has been through a lot. From heart bypass surgery to over 100lbs in weight loss, my mom has always been there for me. She came to all my sports games and has always supported me through whatever I have done. I love my mom and hope that she can, one day, see me doing something that she can be proud of even though I know she is already proud of me. Throughout the past couple of years my mom's health has not been very good but in that time I have seen her grow immensely. She has really come out of her shell and started to interact with people like she never used to. From going out to church and joining bible studies to getting brunch at Denny's, my mom has learnt to really interact and and connect with the people around her. I am so proud of her.
