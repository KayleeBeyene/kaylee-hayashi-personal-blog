My First Post

Kaylee Beyene
July 6, 2017

I’ve never really thought of myself as a writer. Actually, I’ve always claimed to be terrible at it. Writing, speaking, communication of any kind has always been difficult for me. Just as people tend to say they are just naturally bad at math, I used to say that I was just naturally bad at writing. I can see now that that line of reasoning is bollocks. Just like anything else we get taught in school, becoming good at writing takes practice. It happens that some individuals are naturally worse at it than others and some are naturally better. But really, after a certain level of competence, getting good at writing just takes practice. I am trying to start a career in television and radio so I need to learn how to communicate effectively. I need to learn how to speak even if I don’t think I have anything to say. And so I hope posting in this blog on a regular basis will help me to improve my writing skills. Thanks for reading and any feedback is welcome!
